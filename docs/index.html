ABOUT THE EVENT

Energy Day is the coveted research showcasing and industry networking event of the Department of Energy Science and Engineering at Indian Institute of Technology Bombay. It is an opportunity for energy sector stakeholders to learn about the state-of-the-art R&D related to energy systems being undertaken by the department. The event is extensively attended by engineering professionals and industry executives in the energy domain.

FAQs

 What is the objective of Energy Day?

Energy Day seeks to present the current World Energy Scenario, technology and research going on in Energy sector to Industries and Students. It demonstrates the research by students of Energy Science and Engineering Department at IIT Bombay and conducts discussions on trending topics in Energy Sector. Anyone with matching interests should definitely attend this event take part in achieving sustainable development in our country and whole world.

 Is the registration free for Energy Day?

Registration is free for attending Energy Day. To register, simply click on the Register Button and fill out the form.

Note: IIT Bombay will not be able to provide accommodation or reimburse any travel and accommodation expenses.

 Who should attend Energy Day?

The event aims to familiarize the industry and society with the current state of energy technology research going on in IIT Bombay. Its target audience is

> Industries interested in learning about the state-of-the-art R&D related to energy systems being undertaken by the Department of Energy Science and Engineering, IIT Bombay.

> Students of IIT Bombay who are interested and willing to explore and learn about Energy Scenario and technology present in the world.

 Where can I find information about the event venue and schedule?

The website's Event Details page provides information regarding the schedule and venue. Furthermore, a booklet containing the timings and venues of the events such as Guest Lecture, Panel Discussion etc. will be provided to attendees on the day of the event.

 How can I stay updated about the Energy Day?

In order to stay updated about Energy Day, you can visit our website regularly and follow our social media pages for event updates.

SCHEDULE

The Energy Day 2024 Event is scheduled on 6 - 7th of April.

Energy Day24 is organized by the Department of Energy Science and Engineering (DESE) at IIT Bombay, with the primary objective of promoting awareness of the latest advancements in energy technology research being carried out within the department. The event endeavours to establish a link between the energy industry and the academic community by showcasing the research projects undertaken by our students. Energy Day features oral and poster presentations by our department's research scholars, highlighting their innovative research findings. The event also serves as a forum to identify potential research areas relevant to the industry and facilitate technology transfer.
